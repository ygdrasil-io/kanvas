## Scope

KFONT-M1-004 adds a repo-native fixture manifest for bundled and planned
generated font sources. The manifest is evidence plumbing only: it records
fixture IDs, source kind, file path or generator ID, provenance, license facts,
hash/length when real bytes exist, face count, intended coverage tags,
normative status, remaining gates, and report labels.

## Planned Generated Gates

The TTC face-index, malformed directory, and missing-required-table rows are
`PLANNED_GENERATED`. They record generator IDs, source parameters, provenance,
face-count intent, `font.fixture.generated-bytes-missing`, and remaining gates.
They are not normative binary evidence until deterministic bytes and refusal
dumps are checked in and reviewed.

The host-scanned row is `NON_NORMATIVE` and keeps `font.source.host-dependent`.
It has no hash or byte length because bytes are not captured.
